Why Veras?

Veras is derived from the Latin word for truth. It is a name chosen with intention — grounding the company in a commitment to transparency, accuracy, and integrity.

Veras Technologies exists at the intersection of intelligent software and public safety, building tools that help communities, civic organizations, and law enforcement agencies operate with greater clarity and confidence.

In a field where trust is everything, the name Veras is both a promise and a standard.

04  /  Company

About Veras Technologies

Veras Technologies is an AI and intelligent software company built at the intersection of technology and public safety. Based in the United States and serving government agencies, civic organizations, and public safety professionals, we develop intelligent software solutions that address some of the most critical challenges in community safety and law enforcement.

Our products leverage artificial intelligence, machine learning, and real-time data to give agencies the clarity and capability they need to act decisively and confidently. A growing suite of purpose-built solutions — from geographic intelligence platforms to AI-powered recognition systems and conversational government assistants.

At Veras Technologies, we believe that technology should serve people — not the other way around. That belief shapes how we design our products, how we engage with our partners, and how we measure success. We are not building technology for its own sake. We are building it because safer, more informed communities are possible, and because the people working every day to protect those communities deserve tools that are as sophisticated and dependable as they are.

We are a company in motion — small by design, ambitious by nature, and driven by a clear sense of purpose. Every product we ship and every partnership we form is guided by a single question: does this make communities safer?
